Broken Records

Broken Records

In 2018, Kenya's Eliud Kipchoge wore custom Vaporflys when he broke the world record for completing a marathon at 2:01:39, 78 seconds better then the previous best time set 50 years ago. Brigid Kosgei, also of Kenya, had Vaporflys on her feet when she set a new world marathon record for women at 2:14:04 in 2019. Also that year, Uganda's Joshua Cheptegei set the world road race 10k record at 26:38. In 2020 he finished in world record breaking time for a 5k run at 12:51. Japan's Mariko Yugeta became the first woman over 60 to finish a marathon in less than three hours at 2:59:15 in 2019. In 2020, Ethopia's Ababel Yeshaneh set the women's world record for half-marathon at 1:04:31. All wore Vaporflys.
